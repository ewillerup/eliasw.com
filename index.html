ELIAS WILLERUP

Hello! My name is Elias Willerup. I am a game developer. Welcome to my portfolio.

Professionally, I have six years of experience working on games through a startup called Blocksmith and through my degree. I played in a band where I mixed a five-track EP and an eight-track album.

I graduate in 2023 from Boise State University with a degree in Games and Interactive Media Mobile, or GIMM for short.

Audio production is my specialty. I love how a well designed soundscape can be intensely immersive, or how a well mixed song illudes that the band are in the room with me.

I mix and compose music for videogames and performance. I design and record custom sounds through foley and synthesis. I program audio in Unity and I'm learning to use the audio middleware Wwise.
